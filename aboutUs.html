Ambrosia Hair & Beauty Salon

Looking good doesn’t mean you have to spend big bucks. Simply visit Ambrosia Hair & Beauty Salon, where you’ll discover a dedicated team of hairdressers and beauticians on hand to cater to your every need. At Ambrosia our motto is ‘our prices bring you in, our quality brings you back’- and at our one stop shops we cater to the whole family, providing an exceptional array of hair and beauty services. You’ll be under the care of experienced, stylists and beauticians who can tailor a look to meet your individual requirements- whether you’re after a completely new style or your usual cut and colour. To care for your hair at home, we have a extensive range of hair care products. At Ambrosia we also provide a full range of beauty services including threading, eyelash and eyebrow tinting, waxing, nails and soothing facials which will give your skin a youthful glow. We believe a waxing appointment should not take hours or cost you an arm and a leg, and our waxing specialists aim to provide the best service in town. Ambrosia really is the only name you need to remember when it comes to all your hair and beauty requirements!
